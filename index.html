Eligibility:
  • • Students from any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts) can apply. No prior background in psychology or science is required.
  • • Students require a minimum of 50% or 5 CGPA in Grade 12 or equivalent qualifying examination from any recognized board.
  • • Students must submit any of the following aptitude test scores: RSAT / SAT / CUET.
  • • Students must qualify the interview round.
Admission Process:
Step 1- Apply Online — Visit the website and submit your application form.
Step 2- Entrance Test (RSAT / Scholarship Test) — Schedule your RSAT online on the portal.
Step 3- Interview — if shortlisted, schedule and attend your online interview.
Step 4- Offer Letter — Shortlisted students will receive the admission offer (typically within 3 working days after successful evaluation).
Step 5- Secure Seat — Confirm admission and complete payment/acceptance formalities within 10 days to secure your seat.
The Rishihood Scholastic Aptitude Test (RSAT) is designed to assess student’s aptitude, critical thinking, and problem-solving.The test includes a video-based response and sections on Quantitative Aptitude and Psychometric Assessment.
